Hardware

The parts used are entirely dependent on what you wish to build. This is the parts list for the robot that we built.

  • Galileo Board
  • Motor Shield
  • Makeblock Robotics Kit
  • Wireless Adapter
  • Galileo Battery
    • Galileo requires 2A at 5V
  • USB to Barrel Plug
  • Motor Battery
    • Ensure you motor shield can handle the voltage you will provide.
  • Deans connector
    • The leads will need to be stripped to connect to the motor shield.
  • Battery Charger
  • USB Hub
  • Micro Fridge
